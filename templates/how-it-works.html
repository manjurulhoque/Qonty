Getting Started

Starting your campaign is easy:

  • Sign up for a free account
  • Click "Create Campaign"
  • Fill in your campaign details
  • Add images and videos
  • Set your funding goal and deadline

Campaign Tips

Tips for a successful campaign:

  • Tell a compelling story
  • Use high-quality images
  • Set a realistic funding goal
  • Engage with your supporters
  • Share updates regularly

Funding Process

Understanding the funding process:

  • Campaigns run for a set time period
  • Supporters can donate any amount
  • Progress is tracked in real-time
  • Funds are released after campaign ends
  • Platform fee is only 5%
